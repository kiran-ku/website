Our Products

  • Mantis

    " A mission critical, information system that operates in the absence of servers or an established infrastructure. MANTIS works offline while analyzing local data that allows multiple users to communicate with each other in the field over a peer-to-peer communication protocol. "

  • Mongoose

    " Mongoose is a minefield recording observation and monitoring system that aids in detecting and planning automated and manual minefields. It has the ability to share information with multiple users in the field over a peer to peer communication protocol even in the absence of GPS "

  • Precision Guidance kit

    " A precision guided kit for 105mm and 155mm artillery shells and 120mm mortar munition that reduce the number of munitions fired to engage a target. Standard unguided shells and mortars have been proven to be an inefficient solution to deploy in combat due to obscured line of sight and particularly blanket firing. "

  • PINPOINT

    " Introducing India's first CORS(Continiously Operating Reference Station) system for precision positioning. It enhances coordinate approach by a few centimeters both, horizontally and vertically. The network allows equipment, machinery and technology to accurately determine coordinates for positioning and guidance apps. "

  • MARG

    " We are developing a compact, low cost, survey grade GNSS receiver that has(Multi-constellation support (IRNSS, GPS, Galileo, GLONASS, BeiDou), Ability to track up to 4 constellations at a time, Inbuilt SBAS and RTK support (optional), Online and offline maps, 4G/3G/GPRS cellular network support). "
